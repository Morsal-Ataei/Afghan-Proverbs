با یک گل بهار نمی‌شود

One flower does not bring spring.

It means one person alone can't change everything.

آب که یک‌جا بماند می‌گندد

Water stagnates when it stands still.

Staying still or inactive leads to problems.

سیلی نقد به از حلوای نسیه

A slap now is better than sweets later.

It's better to face a harsh truth now than false hopes.

هر که باد می‌کارد، طوفان درو می‌کند

He who sows wind reaps storm.

Bad actions lead to worse consequences.
